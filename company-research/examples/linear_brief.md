## Linear

**TL;DR**: Linear is a project management tool for software teams, known for speed and design quality, now used by ~10,000 companies including OpenAI and Vercel. Recently raised Series C and expanding into broader product workflows.

### Overview

Linear builds issue tracking and project management software designed specifically for engineering teams. Unlike generic tools like Jira, Linear emphasizes speed, keyboard-driven UX, and opinionated workflows. They charge per-user subscriptions ($8-16/user/month) and have grown primarily through word-of-mouth among engineers.

## Why this example is good

Note the qualities to replicate:

- **TL;DR is concrete**: not "Linear is a great project management tool" but "used by ~10,000 companies including OpenAI and Vercel"
- **Signals are dated and sourced**: every signal has a date AND a URL
- **Signals connect to "so what"**: not just "they hired a VP Marketing" but "first marketing hire suggests GTM transition"
- **Bottom line synthesizes**: not just listing facts but telling the reader what the pattern means
- **Tone is neutral**: no "amazing company" puffery, no negative either
- **Scannable structure**: section headers, tables, bullets — readable in 60 seconds
