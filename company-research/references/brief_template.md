# Company Brief Template

Use this exact structure when outputting a company brief. Variable parts in `{braces}`.

---

## {Company Name}

**TL;DR**: {1-2 sentences capturing what the company is and one notable current fact}

### Overview

{2-3 sentences describing what the company does, who they serve, and how they make money. Plain English, no marketing-speak.}

### At a glance

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Legal name** | {Full legal name} |
| **Founded** | {Year} |
| **HQ** | {City, Country} |
| **Headcount** | {Range: 1-10 / 11-50 / 51-200 / 201-500 / 501-1000 / 1000+} |
| **Business model** | {B2B / B2C / B2B2C / Marketplace} |
| **Industry** | {Specific sub-industry, not just "Tech"} |
| **Funding stage** | {Seed / Series A-Z / Public / Bootstrapped / Unknown} |
| **Last known valuation** | {$X with date, or "Not public"} |
| **Key products** | {2-4 products comma-separated} |

### Recent signals (last 90 days)

🟢 **{Signal type}** — {Date}
{1-2 sentence summary of what happened and why it matters}
🔗 [Source]({url})

🟢 **{Signal type}** — {Date}
{Summary}
🔗 [Source]({url})

{Add 3-7 signals total. Omit this section if no signals found, write "No notable signals detected in last 90 days based on public sources."}

### Key people

**{Name}** — {Role}
{1 sentence on background or recent context}

**{Name}** — {Role}
{Background}

{2-4 people total. Omit section if not relevant to user's context.}

### Bottom line

{1-2 sentences synthesizing the most important takeaway for the reader given their context. This is where you add value beyond facts.}

### Sources

1. {Source 1 URL}

3. {...}

---

## Notes for the writer

- Replace ALL `{braces}` with actual content
- Use 🟢 emoji for signals (or 🟡 for neutral, 🔴 for concerning)
- Keep total length 500-800 words for quick mode, 1200-1500 for deep mode
- Tables render in markdown — preserve exact pipe alignment
- Source URLs should be FULL URLs (https://...)
- Dates as "Month YYYY" or "DD Month YYYY"
